Rules


The game is comprised of the following pieces:

Player, Enemies and Bonuses.

The Player:

The Player is a white circle and located at the center of the screen.

The Player dies when an Enemy hits him.

The Player may shoot Projectiles by clicking / pressing on the screen, in order to hit Enemies, which in turn will destroy them.

The Enemies:

The Enemies are colorful circles that randomly spawn at the edges of the screen.

When an Enemy hits the Player, the game is over.

The Enemies vary in size:

When a Projectile hits a small* Enemy, they both are destroyed. Grants 100 Points.

When a Projectile hits a big* Enemy, the Enemy shrink and the Projectile is destroyed. Grants 250 Points.

The Bonuses:

There are 6 types of Bonuses: Slow, Sharp Shooter, Explosive Rounds, Extra Life, Nuke and Skull.

Each Bonus is a hollow square, with a different color.

Course of Game:

The game start on Level 1, with 1 Life, and 0 Points in Score.

The Player is at the center of the screen, while Enemies begin to spawn at the edges of the screen every second and slowly move towards the Player.

When the Player hits an Enemy, 100 Points are added to the Score.

When the Player destroys an Enemy, 250 Points are added to the Score.

For each 10000 earned, the Level will increase by 1.

Each Level increase will make the Enemies spawn more frequently and make them move faster towards the Player.

The game ends when the Player has 1 Life left, and an Enemy hits the Player, or the Player shoots a Projectile that hits the Skull Bonus.
